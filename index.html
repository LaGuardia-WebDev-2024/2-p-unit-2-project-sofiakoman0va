Travel to Equestria

Equestria is the main setting of the My Little Pony Friendship is Magic franchise. Equestria is inhabited by magical ponies and other talking creatures, such as griffons and dragons. Other animals and creatures also live in Equestria.

Visit the many locations of Equestria

    Ponyville

    Ponyville is a town in Equestria, the main setting of the series My Little Pony Friendship is Magic, and home to the leading characters, the Mane 6. There are many places to visit in Ponyville, such as: Carousel Boutique, Sugarcube Corner, Sweet Apple Acres, Castle of Friendship, and so much more.

    Canterlot

    Canterlot is the capital city of Equestria and the location of the royal palace, where Princess Celestia and Princess Luna reside. It is a beautiful city built into a mountain, known for its rich culture, architecture, and as a center of learning and the royal court.

    Crystal Empire

    The Crystal Empire is a region of northern Equestria. It is ruled by Princess Cadance and Shining Armor
